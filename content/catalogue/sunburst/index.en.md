An area-based chart that uses a polar coordinate layout, placing the parent at the center, its children on the surrounding ring, and grandchildren on the next outer ring. Data values are represented by angles showing relative proportions within each hierarchical level. It is called a "sunburst" because its appearance resembles an exploding sun.
