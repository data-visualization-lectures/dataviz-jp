This representation addresses a shortcoming of word clouds -- that the original character length of words affects their visual size, making accurate numerical reading difficult. Circles are placed behind each word, and the area of each circle represents the numerical value. While word font size does not encode the value, words may be drawn at several different sizes for classified grouping. The circles are typically distributed evenly around the center point.
